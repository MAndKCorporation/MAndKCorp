Our Journey & Founding

How three visionary directors united to redefine corporate mobility and fleet logistics in South Africa.

The Genesis of M AND K Corporation

Founded on a shared vision to bring structural efficiency, safety, and modern scaling models to South Africa's transport sector, M AND K Corporation (Pty) Ltd emerged from a simple realization: the gap between asset management, driver empowerment, and corporate logistics required a more integrated, professional framework.

Established formally in May 2026 by three direct stakeholders, the company was built to bridge the disconnect between localized e-hailing networks and enterprise-level logistics requirements. Moving fast from conceptual strategies to official incorporation, we established our roots firmly within Cape Town, setting out to serve corporate clients and operators across the Western Cape with an unyielding standard of professional excellence.

What We Do

M AND K Corporation operates as an agile, Level 1 B-BBEE corporate mobility partner specializing in two core pillars:

  • Strategic Fleet Partnerships: Bridging the gap between vehicle asset placement and professional driver opportunities. We provide sustainable, high-yield frameworks for operators while offering robust fleet options to meet scalable demands.
  • Corporate Logistics & Logistics: Delivering point-to-point corporate staff transport and small package courier services using an optimized, high-efficiency fleet.
