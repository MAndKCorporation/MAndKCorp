E-Hailing Partnerships

Drive to own. No strict credit checks. Built for Uber and Bolt platforms.

At M AND K Corporation, we understand that traditional vehicle finance can be an uphill battle. That’s why we offer specialized Vehicle Rental Lease Agreements turning into a flexible Rent-to-Own program. Our contracts are structured explicitly as non-credit agreements, providing dedicated drivers a direct route to vehicle ownership without conventional financing hurdles.

Program Highlights

  • Platform Ready: Fully compliant and optimized for Uber and Bolt systems.
  • Non-Credit Agreement: Evaluation is based on your earning potential and driving track record, not your classic credit score.
  • Path to Ownership: Your weekly lease installments bring you closer to owning the vehicle entirely.
