Corporate Fleet Driver Recruitment

Join our team of professional drivers operating our corporate logistics fleet.

At M AND K Corporation, we take pride in delivering elite staff transport and parcel logistics to our business partners. To maintain our high standard of reliability and safety, we are looking to recruit dedicated, highly responsible, and customer-focused drivers to join our growing corporate fleet operations.

Minimum Application Requirements

To ensure eligibility for our corporate positions, all incoming candidates must completely satisfy the following baseline standards:

  • Valid Driver's License: Clean driving history with proper operating credentials.
  • Professional Driving Permit (PrDP): Must hold a valid, active PrDP for passenger/goods transport.
  • Professional Conduct: Strong time management and high-quality customer care values.
  • Experience: Solid driving history within Cape Town areas is highly beneficial.
  • Comprehensive Background: Willingness to undergo vehicle operation assessments and security checks.

Why Drive With M AND K Corporation?

We supply modern, fuel-efficient passenger cars (5 and 7-seaters) that are completely backed by comprehensive passenger liability and commercial cargo insurance shields, offering you a structured and safe environment to build a stable professional career.
